Artificial Intelligence - A Beautiful Enigma!

Admin
Apr 19, 2018 / 6 minutes to read

Have your ever wondered if you wake up everyday, you feel that you have created something beautiful and cherish the ecstasy of creation?

Something that Mercedes Benz would have felt when its S class autonomous car adorned the streets in the mid 90s for the first time, or something what IBM would have felt when its brain child rattled the fortress in Gary Kasparov with its razor sharp moves.

Artificial Intelligence, in recent times has turned out to be more than what it was earlier. Unlike the world back then, getting flabbergasted by the works of visionaries like Issac Asimov and Arthur.C.Clarke isn't a strong routine of tech-geeks anymore. They had far evolved to get right into the field and realise and shape those ahead-of-their-time science-fiction to a stark yet intimidating reality. The claim of artificial intelligence 2020 seems to be more than just a plain assertion. The idea of imparting human like intelligence to machines, driving them to make decisions to achieve goals on their own has grown up and is only expected to grow more to transform the world. The transformation as a whole is still a two sided coin, subjective to whether it would be beneficial or otherwise.

The Underlying Context of AI

Artificial intelligence is different from what the motion pictures flexibly exaggerate over the top. It wasn't an overnight phenomenon that happened all of a sudden. It has long been there for ages, ever since the dire need for solving complex problems without fearing time that drove the advent of the ancestral members of the machine age like the calculators and difference engines. It is just that, in the recent times, it has grown to such an extent that it actually feels like there is some real intelligence being created.

Non Sentience and AI for the Trivial

Artificial intelligence on a higher level can be crudely broken down firstly as weak AI systems with no sentience and with restricted capabilities to accomplish a very specific narrow task. They can also be characterised by their near-zero dependency on memory and an extremely narrow intelligence.

An investor might find the prediction engines equipped with basic machine learning algorithms, darlings to be sided with. But, at the same time, it should be of no wonder that one finds an automated customer-care voice call so draggy or when Natasha or Siri, after a point of time become extremely monotonous pals to interact with.

Versatile and Generic AI for the Sophisticated

Counteracting on the adversities, emerged the next level of machine intelligence or like what the tech geeks call, the Artificial General Intelligence where systems have a slight higher tinge of sophistication. The ability to solve wide range of tasks learning from what it has been trained on.

The training here is done using comparatively complex learning models, that tend to look beyond the narrow intelligence horizon. For starters, Perceptron and Neural networks (basic to higher up the hierarchy like convolutional or the recurrent Neural networks) with strong vice-grip son mathematical functions(convolution in the case of convolutional neural nets) to attempt mimicking human memory systems can be considered best examples.

Thus said, artificial general systems with their limited to strong memory dependencies and slightly decent sentience, ultimately aim to perform any general intellectual task that a generic human intelligence can perform.

The learning models have become far more improvised with extremely sophisticated reinforcement learning algorithms based upon human behavioural psychology, a self rewarding, self-punishment system used in games like DoTA and FIFA et.al where learning through mistakes from early to late gaming is considered significant and proficient.

The Promises of AI and AI 2020

In a fast moving world that is so dynamic, a personal assistant robot that can understand the material requirements of a master could go a long way. When perfection is the ultimate order of the day, machines with no or very less room for errors become the obvious go-tos. The demand has been so enormous that one would never be surprised with the famous artificial intelligence 2020 claim.

Elaborating on which, AI is expected to tremendously dominate the employment sectors, as it looks to ascertain almost the entire digital world's reliance to it in the near future, in almost all the major financial and economical powerhouses.

Super AI and An Enigmatic Magnificence Beyond

With the question of order effectively addressed, there comes another very important question on survival of the human race. In another way, the questions are

  • How far advanced can AI get?
  • How deep can sentience get into a machine?

Over the years, it has been evident that a machine can perform tasks that are near impossible for a human. Yet, the question is what sets both apart and the quest to answer how human mind is still able to take the machines successfully is a definite cliff-hanger. Tasks simple for a human are still a far cry for a machine.

The fact that robot doctors are still a research material concretely justifies the above assertion of insecurity and risk. Beyond all these, there lies a milliondollar debate of how threatening or fascinating can Artificial consciousness be, that founds the practicality Super AI systems.

The idea of a machine becoming self-aware of its own reality and man playing god by creating the absolutely complete intelligence all by himself poses this very suspicion of human survival and the expected aftermath is still a very big enigma!
